About

I believe that our planet has always something to tell us, either is a beautiful sunset that fill us with serenity and peace, the unusual and deep gaze of an animal or a person whose story tells by itself without words. We learn from everything we see. But sometimes we are not that open to the little details life give us. And that is where the joy is.

I was born in Monterrey, Mexico and since little I got amazed by two things: Stories and Nature. I loved to visit the beautiful destinations of my country when travelling with my parents, imagining thousand of stories with everything I saw. Eventually I found a way to describe this admiration for places through photography in my early years of college. After obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Communication and Advertising, I worked in the publishing industry in a well known magazine for some years, then I took the courage to turn my hobby into my vocation. So I started traveling.

That is my objective, to find, capture and share in a photograph a beautiful moment that tells it's story, that maybe, hanged in your living room as a Fine Art print, in the pages of a magazine or shared by a friend on social media, you can take a break from the noisy world and observe a powerful image that reminds you, why stay mad or sad when it is so much beauty in the world? And help brands and people to communicate effectively their ideas through photography.

Cause, yes, a picture is worth a thousand words.
